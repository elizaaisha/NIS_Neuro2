Clinical Outcomes of MS in CVD

Analysis for RCOP NIS Neuro2

Author
Affiliation

Eliza Aisha

Dow University of Health Sciences

Preamble:

  • Reference Papers:

    • Sidhu et al. 2022

  • Study Objective: Examine the impact of Multiple Sclerosis (MS) on in-hospital mortality and length of stay among inpatient admissions with a principal diagnosis of cerebrovascular (CVD)

  • Data Source: Cross-sectional analysis of the National Inpatient Sample (NIS) from 2018 to 2020.

  • Patient Selection: Included all inpatient admissions with a principal diagnosis of cerebrovascular disease.

  • CVD + MS Categories:

    • CVD with MS

    • CVD without MS

  • Primary Outcomes:

    • In-hospital all-cause mortality

    • Total hospital length of stay (days)

    • Total charge, inflation adjusted to 2020 ($)

  • Software: All analyses were performed using R Version 4.4.1 (R Foundation for Statistical Computing, Vienna, Austria)
